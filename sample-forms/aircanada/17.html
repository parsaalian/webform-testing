Check-in tips

  • Booking reference: 6 letters and/or numbers (e.g.: ABC123)
  • Aeroplan ID: 9 numbers (e.g.: 123456789)
  • AC employee number: 8 letters and/or numbers (e.g.: AC012345)
  • Ticket number: 13 numbers (e.g.: 0141234567890)
